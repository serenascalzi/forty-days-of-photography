Behind the Camera

Serena Scalzi

I am a marketing manager with over ten years of experience, including digital marketing, graphic design, and web development. I am also a graduate of Indiana University, Otis College of Art and Design, and PunchCode.

In addition to my marketing and design work, I am a published photographer who has trained in darkroom and digital photography. I also have a passion for instant photography.

Forty Days of Photography is my latest project, a collection that documents my travels throughout the American Southwest. All of the photographs included in this project were shot using an iPhone 6 and edited with Hipstamatic and Photoshop.
